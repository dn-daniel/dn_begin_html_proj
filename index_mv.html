The Best (Anime) Movies According to Denny

My top 3 Anime movies of all time

Princess Mononoke

Favorite Miyazaki film by far, excellent characters, compelling plot, themes of nature vs industrialization and humanity, conflict and resolution, and an excellent sweeping soundtrack

Ghost in the Shell (or GitS 2: Innocence)

Classic Cyberpunk Anime Film that started a franchise. Techno setting, philosophical questions on our human nature, and action scenes, plus the traditional Japanese inspired Soundtrack

Summer Wars

Great summer film (pun intended), great cast, and themes of family, gaming, digital life, escalating plot, action scenes, hype moments, etc.
